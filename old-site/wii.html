Nintendo Wii

The Wii is the most interesting next-gen console. It has a great thing that diferences it from its competitors, the Wiimote. This device is the controller of the console. It has some accelerometers and an optical sensor which you can use to know the inclination or acceleration of the controller and point to the screen. Apart from that, you can plug in some joypads with extra buttons and sensors.
