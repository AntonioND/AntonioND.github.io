Space Adventure

I made this game specially for the 2008-09 drunkencoders winter compo. I didn't win anything, but I don't care. I used Nitro Engine to make it, and this helped me to fix some strange bugs. Anyway, it was interesting to make a modified ARM7 core.

Instructions

You have to patch this game with DLDI if your flashcard doesn't do it by itself. This game will create a small file in the root of your flashcard with your records and configuration. You can configure the controls as you like (except the stylus, which will always move the ship).

There are two secrets in the menu, hints:
-Wind can move the snow.
-Credits' lower screen isn't as useless as you think...
