GiiBii

Just another GameBoy/GameBoy Color emulator for Wii. I didn't code it to compete with the other emulators around there, I just coded it because I wanted to prove myself I could do it, this is my first emulator. Here you are some crappy pictures taken with my mobile phone:

Instructions

Copy the contents of the apps folder in the rar file into the folder apps on your sd. Copy your roms to the sd. You can find some free roms here:

http://www.pdroms.de/files/gameboy/

If that roms aren't the ones you are looking for, sorry, I can't help you.
